Scope

✓ In scope

  • Aegis API surface at aegisagent.in / eu.aegisagent.in
  • staging.aegisagent.in (synthetic data only)
  • Aegis SDKs on PyPI (aegis-aevf, aegis-anthropic, aegis-openai, aegis-bedrock, aegis-langchain)
  • Aegis container images we publish (per the Customer Security Package SBOM)
  • Documentation vulnerabilities that demonstrate a real attack

✗ Out of scope

  • Other tenants' data — report and we'll reproduce; do NOT pivot
  • Social engineering against employees or suppliers
  • Physical attacks against infrastructure
  • Denial-of-service attacks
  • Findings in third-party SaaS (Clerk, Stripe, AWS) — report to that vendor
  • Best-practice warnings without demonstrable security impact

Safe harbor

We will not pursue civil action or initiate law-enforcement action against you for good-faith research that complies with this policy. This includes accidental DMCA / CFAA / equivalent claims — we waive them. Make a good-faith effort to avoid data exfiltration, service degradation, and access to other tenants' data, and notify us immediately if you encounter any of those during research.

If a third party brings action against you for research conducted under this policy, contact us — we will intervene in good faith to clarify that the research was authorised by Aegis.
